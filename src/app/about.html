WE ARE PingCAP

PingCAP is focused on developing distributed NewSQL and is the team building TiDB, an open-source ditributed NewSQL database. PingCAP is headquartered in Beijing. The team members are senior engineers and architects who used to work at the infrastructure teams in Twitter.com, JD.com, Baidu.com, Qihoo 360, Sougou.com, Wandou Labs, etc. PingCAP is dedicated to building the database of the future and provide enterprises with a perfect database solution for the horizontal scaling, true Geo-Redundancy and Multi-active, and distributed transactions.

OUR STORY

I was often bedevilled by distributed databases in my previous jobs as an infrastructure engineer. I just cannot find a database that is horizontally scalable and ensures distributed transactions at the same time. Later on, after discussions with the peers from other companies, I found that I am not alone and that this is a common issue that everybody is facing. So several of us sat down and took a serious look at this issue and decided to solve it once and for all - to develop a database of the future, hence PingCAP.
